📝 Tenses – Quick Notes

Present Simple  ·  Past Simple  ·  Present Continuous

1
Present Simple
Things that happen always / usually / every day
🕐 Time Words
always usually often sometimes never every day on Mondays
📐 Formula
✅ I / You / We / They → verb
✅ He / She / It → verb + s / es
❌ I / You / We / They → do not + verb
❌ He / She / It → does not + verb
💡 Examples
✅ Positive❌ Negative
I eat rice every day. I do not eat bread.
She plays football on Saturdays. She does not play tennis.
They go to school by bus. He does not like cats.
2
Past Simple
Things that already happened (finished)
🕐 Time Words
yesterday last night last week ago in 2020 just now
📐 Formula
✅ Subject → verb + ed  (regular)
✅ Subject → irregular verb  (see list below)
❌ Subject → did not + verb
💡 Examples
✅ Positive❌ Negative
I walked to school yesterday. I did not walk today.
She ate pizza last night. She did not eat vegetables.
They went to the park last week. He did not go to school.
3
Present Continuous
Things happening RIGHT NOW
🕐 Time Words
now right now at the moment look! listen!
📐 Formula
✅ I → am + verb-ing
✅ He / She / It → is + verb-ing
✅ You / We / They → are + verb-ing
❌ → am / is / are + not + verb-ing
💡 Examples
✅ Positive❌ Negative
I am eating now. I am not sleeping.
She is reading a book. She is not watching TV.
They are playing outside. We are not going home yet.

🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ She go to school. → ✅ She goes to school.  (he/she/it needs +s)
❌ I am eat now. → ✅ I am eating now.  (continuous needs –ing)
❌ She goed home. → ✅ She went home.  (irregular verb!)
❌ He did not went. → ✅ He did not go.  (after did not → base form)
💡 Trick: Look for the TIME WORD first — it tells you which tense to use!
